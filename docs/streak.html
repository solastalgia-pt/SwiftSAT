How It Works

Instead of quizzing you randomly, Streak Mode uses spaced repetition — words you find hard come back sooner, words you know well come back later (days or weeks). Same method behind Anki and Duolingo.

A Study Session, Step by Step

  1. You see a flashcard — the front shows an SAT word
  2. Think about the definition, then press Space (or tap the card) to flip it
  3. The back reveals the full definition and an example sentence
  4. Rate how well you knew it:
ButtonMeaningWhen to use
Again I had no idea Totally blank
Hard I kind of knew it Vague or wrong
Good I got it Correct but took effort
Easy Too easy Instant recall
  • Again → back in 10 minutes (same session)
  • Good → back in 1 day, then 6 days, then longer and longer
  • Easy → skips ahead even further

The Streak 🔥

  • Your daily goal is 10 cards
  • Study every day → your streak number goes up
  • Miss a day → streak resets to 0

Era Unlocking

  • You start with the Taylor Swift debut era only
  • Once you've seen every card in an era, the next era unlocks
  • Eras go from Easy → Medium → Advanced vocabulary
